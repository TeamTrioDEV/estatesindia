Project Arputhalaya


Arputhalaya:
We our promoting independent bungalows in our layout "Arputhalaya" which is sprawling 3.5 acres at Vengaivasal, near Medavakkam.

This is our dream project because with the acute shortage of space in the city and the escalating land costs building independent houses for our clients in such close proximity to the city will not be a easy task to repeat.


About the Site:
  • Located within 8Km from the IT corridor.
  • Adjacent to Medavakkam and Tambaram and just a Km from Chitalapakkam housing board layout.
  • Site is a elevated plain and hence watter logging is not a problem
  • Sweet water,good soil and a pollution free environment add to the aesthetics of thr site.
